# PMFBY – Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana

## Overview
**Full Name:** Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)
**Launched:** Kharif 2016 by Government of India
**Purpose:** Provide financial support to farmers suffering crop loss due to natural calamities, pests, and diseases. Stabilize farmer income and encourage adoption of modern farming practices.

## Premium Rates (Farmer's Share)
| Season | Crop Type | Farmer Premium |
|---|---|---|
| Kharif | Food & Oilseed crops | **2% of Sum Insured** |
| Rabi | Food & Oilseed crops | **1.5% of Sum Insured** |
| Annual Commercial/Horticultural | All types | **5% of Sum Insured** |

- The remaining premium is shared equally between **State Government and Central Government**.
- Sum Insured = **District-level Scale of Finance** (set by District Level Technical Committee – DLTC).

## Coverage – What Risks are Covered?
1. **Prevented Sowing / Planting Risk** – When widespread calamity prevents farmers from sowing
2. **Standing Crop Loss** – Due to drought, flood, pest/disease, hailstorm, cyclone, fire, lightning
3. **Post-Harvest Losses** – Up to 14 days after harvest for crops kept in cut & spread condition (hailstorm, cyclone, unseasonal rain)
4. **Localized Calamity** – Hailstorm, landslide, inundation affecting isolated farms (individual assessment)
5. **Add-on coverage** (optional): Wild animal attack, cloud burst (state discretion)

## Crops Covered in Andhra Pradesh
- **Kharif:** Paddy, Maize, Cotton, Groundnut, Soybean, Bajra, Red gram, Green gram, Black gram, Chilli, Tobacco
- **Rabi:** Paddy (Rabi), Jowar, Bengalgram, Sunflower, Groundnut
- **Horticultural:** Tomato, Onion, Banana, Mango (selected districts)

## AP Districts – Coverage Areas
All 26 districts of Andhra Pradesh are covered. Major cropping districts:
- **Delta/Coastal:** Krishna, Guntur, East Godavari, West Godavari, NTR – Paddy focus
- **Rayalaseema:** Kurnool, Kadapa, Anantapur, Chittoor – Groundnut, Cotton focus
- **North Coastal:** Visakhapatnam, Vizianagaram, Srikakulam – Paddy, Cashew, Sugarcane

## Enrollment – How to Register
1. **Through Bank Branch** – Visit your nationalized/cooperative bank (for loanee farmers, enrollment is automatic)
2. **Common Service Centre (CSC)** – Can enroll non-loanee farmers
3. **Online Portal:** [pmfby.gov.in](https://pmfby.gov.in) – direct farmer registration
4. **AP Farmer App / Mee Seva** – State-level digital enrollment
5. **Insurance Company Agent** – Empanelled companies visit villages during enrollment drive

> **Non-loanee farmers must specifically opt-in** – coverage is not automatic for them.

## Enrollment Deadlines (Cutoff Dates)
| Season | Deadline |
|---|---|
| **Kharif 2026** | **July 31, 2026** |
| Rabi 2025-26 | December 31, 2025 |

> Do NOT miss the cutoff date. Late enrollment is not accepted.

## Documents Required
1. Aadhaar Card
2. Land Records / Pattadar Passbook / 7/12 extract
3. Bank Passbook (Aadhaar-linked)
4. Sowing certificate (from Village Agricultural Officer – VAO)
5. Mobile number

## Claim Process
1. **Intimation within 72 hours** of crop damage – Notify bank, insurance company, or agriculture department
2. Contact **Crop Insurance App** (PMFBY app) to register loss
3. **Crop Cutting Experiments (CCE)** conducted by government to assess area-level loss
4. For localized/post-harvest losses, **individual survey** is done within 48 hours
5. **Claim Settlement:** Must be completed within **60 days** of harvest/end of season
6. Amount transferred directly to Aadhaar-linked bank account

## How Claim Amount is Calculated
**Claim = (Threshold Yield – Actual Yield) / Threshold Yield × Sum Insured**

- Threshold Yield = Average yield of past 7 years (excluding 2 extreme years)
- If actual district yield falls below threshold → all insured farmers in that unit get claim

## Helpline & Contact
- **National Helpline:** 14447
- **PMFBY Portal:** [pmfby.gov.in](https://pmfby.gov.in)
- **AP Agriculture Department:** Contact district agriculture office
- **Crop Loss Intimation:** Also via SMS/Toll-free to insurance company

## Key Points for AP Farmers
- In AP, **PMFBY is linked with Rythu Bharosa Kendra (RBK)** system for farmer support
- Loanee farmers are **auto-enrolled** – but verify your crop and coverage details at bank
- Always get a **receipt/acknowledgment** after enrollment
- Keep **sowing date records** – needed for prevented sowing claims
