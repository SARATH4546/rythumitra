# Annadata Sukhibhava – AP Farmer Income Support Scheme

## Overview
**Scheme Name:** Annadata Sukhibhava (అన్నదాత సుఖీభవ)
**Launched:** August 2025 by Government of Andhra Pradesh
**Replaced:** YSR Rythu Bharosa scheme (which was paused after government change)
**Purpose:** Provide comprehensive income support to all farmer families in Andhra Pradesh by combining state and central government financial assistance into a single package.
**Official Portal:** [annadathasukhibhava.ap.gov.in](https://annadathasukhibhava.ap.gov.in)

## Financial Benefit – Total ₹20,000 Per Year
| Component | Amount | Source |
|---|---|---|
| State Government Support | ₹14,000/year | AP State Government |
| PM-KISAN Component | ₹6,000/year | Central Government (PM-KISAN) |
| **Total Annual Benefit** | **₹20,000/year** | Combined |

- All money is credited **directly to the farmer's bank account** through Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT)
- Payments are released in **installments** aligned with agricultural seasons (Kharif and Rabi)

## Eligibility
- All **farmer families in Andhra Pradesh** who possess a valid **Pattadar Passbook**
- Both **small/marginal farmers** and other landholding farmers are eligible
- Tenant farmers with valid lease agreements (in some provisions)
- Must be a **resident of Andhra Pradesh**
- Should have an **Aadhaar-linked bank account**

## Who is NOT Eligible
- Farmers who do not possess Pattadar Passbook or valid land documents
- Income Tax payers (as per PM-KISAN central component exclusion)
- Current/former government employees with monthly pension above ₹10,000
- Constitutional post holders (MPs, MLAs, Ministers)
- Institutional landholders

## Documents Required for Registration
1. **Pattadar Passbook** – Primary document (proof of land ownership in AP)
2. **Aadhaar Card** – Mandatory for identity verification and eKYC
3. **Bank Passbook** – Account must be Aadhaar-linked for DBT
4. **Mobile Number** – Linked to Aadhaar for OTP verification
5. **Caste Certificate** (if applicable)
6. **Photograph** (passport size)

## How to Register
1. **Visit Village Secretariat** – The primary registration point in AP
   - Meet the Ward/Village Agriculture Assistant or Volunteer
   - Submit all required documents
   - Enrollment linked with AP Village Secretariat system
2. **Online Portal:** [annadathasukhibhava.ap.gov.in](https://annadathasukhibhava.ap.gov.in)
   - Self-registration for farmers with Aadhaar-linked mobile
3. **Rythu Bharosa Kendra (RBK)** – Local agricultural support centre assists with enrollment
4. **CSC (Common Service Centre)** – Meeseva centres can assist with registration

## eKYC – Mandatory Step
- eKYC must be completed to activate both state and PM-KISAN components
- Can be done at Village Secretariat, CSC/Meeseva centres, or online via Aadhaar OTP
- Biometric authentication also accepted

## Integration with PM-KISAN
- Annadata Sukhibhava integrates seamlessly with PM-KISAN
- The ₹6,000 PM-KISAN component is credited directly from Central Government through the same beneficiary database
- Farmers registered under Annadata Sukhibhava are simultaneously enrolled for PM-KISAN benefits
- eKYC for PM-KISAN is required separately at [pmkisan.gov.in](https://pmkisan.gov.in)

## Payment Schedule
| Installment | Period | Amount (State) |
|---|---|---|
| 1st (Kharif) | May–June | ₹7,000 |
| 2nd (Rabi) | October–November | ₹7,000 |

PM-KISAN payments are additionally credited as per Central Government schedule (3 × ₹2,000).

## Check Beneficiary Status
- Visit [annadathasukhibhava.ap.gov.in](https://annadathasukhibhava.ap.gov.in)
- Enter Aadhaar number or Pattadar Passbook number to verify status and payment history

## Key Points for AP Farmers
- **Pattadar Passbook is compulsory** – Without it, farmer cannot be enrolled
- Scheme covers farmers across all **26 districts of AP**
- Tenant farmers are advised to get their names updated via **ROR (Record of Rights)** for eligibility
- Any grievances can be raised at the **Village Secretariat** or through the AP helpline

## Helpline & Support
- **AP Agriculture Helpline:** 1800-425-2910 (Toll-free)
- **Village Secretariat:** Contact local Volunteer or Ward Sachivalayam Agricultural Assistant
- **Rythu Bharosa Kendra (RBK):** Nearest RBK for on-ground support
