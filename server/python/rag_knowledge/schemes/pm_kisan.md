# PM-KISAN Scheme – Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi

## Overview
**Full Name:** Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)
**Launched:** February 2019 by Government of India
**Purpose:** Provide direct income support to farmer families across India to supplement their financial needs for purchasing agricultural inputs, meeting household expenses, and improving overall livelihood.

## Financial Benefit
- **Total Annual Benefit:** ₹6,000 per year
- **Installment Structure:** 3 installments of ₹2,000 each
  - 1st Installment: April – July
  - 2nd Installment: August – November
  - 3rd Installment: December – March
- Money is directly credited to the farmer's Aadhaar-linked bank account through DBT (Direct Benefit Transfer).
- **23rd Installment:** Released in June 2026, benefiting crores of farmers across India including Andhra Pradesh.

## Eligibility
- All **land-holding farmer families** (husband, wife, and minor children) who own cultivable land.
- Both small and marginal farmers as well as larger landholding farmers are eligible.
- Applicable to farmers owning land in rural and urban areas.

## Who is NOT Eligible (Exclusions)
- Former/current Income Tax payers
- Institutional landholders (trusts, companies, co-operative societies)
- Current/former constitutional post holders (Ministers, MPs, MLAs, Mayors)
- Retired/serving government employees with pension above ₹10,000/month (excluding Multi-Tasking Staff/Class IV/Group D employees)
- Doctors, Engineers, Lawyers, Chartered Accountants and other professionals registered with professional bodies
- Retired pensioners with monthly pension ≥ ₹10,000

## Documents Required
1. **Aadhaar Card** (mandatory – for eKYC and DBT)
2. **Land Records / Pattadar Passbook** (proof of land ownership)
3. **Bank Passbook** (Aadhaar-linked account)
4. **Mobile Number** linked to Aadhaar

## How to Register
1. **Village/Block Agriculture Office** – Visit the local agriculture officer or village level worker (VLW)
2. **Common Service Centre (CSC)** – Nearest CSC can complete registration
3. **Online Portal:** [pmkisan.gov.in](https://pmkisan.gov.in) – self-registration option available
4. Farmers can also register through **Village Secretariat** in Andhra Pradesh

## eKYC – Mandatory Requirement
- eKYC is **compulsory** for all PM-KISAN beneficiaries to continue receiving payments.
- Can be done via:
  - **OTP-based eKYC** on pmkisan.gov.in (Aadhaar-linked mobile required)
  - **Biometric eKYC** at nearest CSC centre
  - **Face Authentication** via PM-KISAN mobile app
- Farmers who miss eKYC may have installments withheld.

## AP-Specific Details
- In **Andhra Pradesh**, PM-KISAN is integrated with the **Annadata Sukhibhava** scheme.
- The ₹6,000 PM-KISAN benefit forms the **central government component** of the combined ₹20,000/year Annadata Sukhibhava package.
- AP farmers must have a valid **Pattadar Passbook** for eligibility verification.
- Registration and eKYC facilitated through AP's **Village Secretariat system**.

## Check Beneficiary Status
- Visit [pmkisan.gov.in](https://pmkisan.gov.in) → "Beneficiary Status"
- Enter Aadhaar number or mobile number to check payment status

## Helpline
- **PM-KISAN Helpline:** 155261 / 011-24300606
- **Email:** pmkisan-ict@gov.in

## Common Issues & Solutions
| Problem | Solution |
|---|---|
| Payment not received | Check eKYC status; verify bank account is Aadhaar-linked |
| Name mismatch | Update Aadhaar name to match land records at local office |
| Land record not verified | Contact local Revenue/Agriculture office |
| New farmer not registered | Visit CSC or Village Secretariat with all documents |
